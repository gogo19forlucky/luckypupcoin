Lucky Pup Fund – Sharing for Stray & Sick Dogs

This project is dedicated to helping dogs in need — whether they are sick or homeless. You can support this fund by purchasing LuckyPup tokens. These tokens represent your generosity and may bring you fortune in the future as the fund grows. If it doesn't, consider it a charitable act for needy dogs.

The token you hold is called "Sharing". When a pup in need is hungry, they may sell this token for food (depending on its current market value).

To donate, simply load SOL into your wallet and press "Buy LuckyPup Token." If you don’t have a wallet, you can sign up free at OKX. If you can’t buy via exchange or wallet, contact our helper to purchase the token for you (may cost slightly more with gas fees).
