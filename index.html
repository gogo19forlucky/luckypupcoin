$LUCKYPUP

The Lucky Pup Fund – Feeding Hope for Pups

This project is dedicated to helping sick or homeless dogs. You can donate to this fund by purchasing the $LUCKYPUP token. Holding the token might bring you luck in the future as the fund grows – or even if it doesn’t, you’ve still helped a pup in need.

Think of it as sharing: When your doggo friend needs help, they can sell the token to buy food (based on the market value at the time).

To donate: Just top up your wallet with SOL and press the button to buy $LUCKYPUP tokens. Don’t have a wallet? Create one here (OKX). Need help? Contact our helper to purchase directly (may include small gas fees).
